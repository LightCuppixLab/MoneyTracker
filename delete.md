## How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the app in two simple steps:

1. Open **Light Money Tracker**
2. Go to **Profile → Delete account**
3. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all associated data will be **permanently and immediately deleted**.

---

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your profile (nickname, currency preference)
- All your transactions (income and expenses)
- All your recurring transactions
- All your custom categories
- All your feedback submitted via "Rate the App"

**No data is retained after deletion.**
